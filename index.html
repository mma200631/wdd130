Lagos, Nigeria

Lagos, Nigeria's largest city and economic hub, is a vibrant metropolis known for its dynamic blend of culture, commerce, and urban energy. Situated along the Atlantic coast, Lagos is home to over 20 million residents, making it one of the fastest-growing cities in the world. The city boasts a rich history, from its colonial past to its role as a thriving center for trade and innovation in West Africa. Lagos features bustling markets, modern skyscrapers, and cultural landmarks, such as the National Museum and Freedom Park, alongside scenic beaches and a thriving arts scene. Despite challenges like traffic congestion, Lagos remains a beacon of resilience and entrepreneurial spirit.
